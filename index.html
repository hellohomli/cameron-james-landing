About · Biography

Cameron James is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist from Kelowna, BC, filtering R&B, hip-hop, and pop through a decade of quiet study. Signed to 100 Collective Records, he's worked with Grammy-winning engineers, landed on Spotify editorials, and built a catalog that rewards patience.
